Visacho menu

Salad

Insalata di Burrata e Pomodorini Confit

Creamy burrata is served with slow-roasted cherry tomatoes,
fresh basil leaves, a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and aged balsamic glaze,
finished with sea salt flakes, cracked black pepper, and toasted pine nuts, accompanied by grilled sourdough crisps.

Insalata di Rucola con Parmigiano e Prosciutto

Fresh arugula is tossed with extra virgin olive oil and lemon juice,
topped with thin slices of Prosciutto di Parma and shavings of Parmigiano Reggiano,
finished with cracked black pepper, sea salt flakes, roasted walnuts, and a drizzle of aged balsamic vinegar.

Starters

Carpaccio di Gamberi Rossi

Hand-sliced red prawns are delicately dressed with Sicilian extra virgin olive oil,
fresh lime zest, sea salt flakes, and pink peppercorns, served with microgreens and a touch of lemon foam.

Tartar di Tonno con Avocado

Fresh bluefin tuna tartare is layered with creamy avocado, extra virgin olive oil,
citrus reduction, chives, and crunchy fennel, finished with Maldon sea salt and a hint of chili oil.

Main Courses

Tagliatelle al Tartufo Nero

Fresh handmade tagliatelle are tossed in a silky butter emulsion
with black truffle shavings, Parmigiano Reggiano, and a touch of aged white wine reduction.

Filetto di Manzo al Barolo

Prime beef fillet is pan-seared and slow-finished in a
rich Barolo wine reduction, served with truffle mashed potatoes, roasted baby vegetables, and rosemary jus.

Desserts

Fondente al Cioccolato con Cuore Caldo

Warm dark chocolate fondant with a molten center,
served with vanilla bean gelato, raspberry coulis, and cocoa crumble.

Torta al Limone di Amalfi

A delicate Amalfi lemon tart with buttery pastry, lemon cream filling,
candied zest, and a light dusting of powdered sugar, served with basil-infused mascarpone cream.
